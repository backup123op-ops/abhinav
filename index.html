What is Funngro?

Funngro is a digital platform operated by Wishbanc Technologies Pvt. Ltd., connecting teens aged 14+ with companies seeking fresh, affordable talent for real-world tasks and projects.

Three Ways to Connect

Teenlancers

Teenagers gain independence through paid freelance projects and practical experience.

Companies

Businesses access young, motivated talent for digital and creative work.

Shelancers

Women professionals find flexible freelancing opportunities to build careers.

What Can You Do?

Content & Creative

Blogging, writing, voice-overs, social media marketing, product reviews.

Digital Tasks

Surveys, app testing, registrations, campus ambassador roles.

Start Earning in 5 Steps

Sign up on the Funngro app
Build your profile
Choose matching projects
Complete work & submit proof
Get paid via wallet & UPI

Learning + Earning

Beyond money, Funngro focuses on skill-building, certificates, and real experience that strengthens future academic and career opportunities.
